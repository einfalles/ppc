The Story of Pie

Pie is a happy food & Pacific Pie always delivers.

P

acific Pie Company was launched in 2009 when husband and wife team Sarah + Chris moved to Portland from Chris’ native Australia. Chris was homesick for all things Aussie, including meat pies, so Sarah started baking them for him…and a business was born!

Sarah and Chris started by selling their pies at local farmer’s markets, and then opened a small cafe in Southeast Portland in 2010.
